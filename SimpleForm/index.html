What is Home??
A home is a dwelling-place used as a permanent or semi-permanent residence for an individual, family, household or several families in a tribe. It is often a house, apartment, or other building, or alternatively a mobile home, houseboat, yurt or any other portable shelter.

Single Family Home A single family attached home is separated from other structures with walls that are extended from the ground to the roof.

Joint Family A joint family is an extended family arrangement prevalent throughout the Indian subcontinent, particularly in India.
